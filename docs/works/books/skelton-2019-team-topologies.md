## Summary

Skelton & Pais make organization part of architecture:
if Conway's Law is inevitable, use it intentionally.

The book introduces:
- 4 team types
- 3 interaction modes
- concept of cognitive load as a design constraint

## Key Ideas

### Team types
- Stream-aligned
- Enabling
- Complicated-subsystem
- Platform

### Interaction modes
- Collaboration
- X-as-a-Service
- Facilitating

### Cognitive load
Teams should own a domain that fits in their heads. Platforms
and enabling teams reduce load and accelerate flow.
