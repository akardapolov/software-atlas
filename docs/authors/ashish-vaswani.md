## Biography

Ashish Vaswani is a computer scientist and researcher who co-authored
the landmark 2017 paper "Attention Is All You Need" while at Google
Brain. This paper introduced the **transformer** architecture — the
neural network design that underpins virtually every modern large
language model, from GPT to BERT to Claude and Gemini.

Vaswani's work replaced recurrence and convolution with self-attention,
enabling parallel training over long sequences and unlocking the scaling
that defines modern LLMs.

## Key Contributions

### Transformer Architecture (2017)

Vaswani and his co-authors at Google Brain demonstrated that attention
mechanisms alone, without recurrence or convolution, achieve
state-of-the-art results on machine translation while training
significantly faster. The transformer became the foundation of the
modern LLM era.

### Subsequent Work

After the transformer paper, Vaswani continued work on efficient
attention mechanisms and large-scale language modeling. He co-founded
Adept AI, a company building AI agents for software workflows.
