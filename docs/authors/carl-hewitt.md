## Biography

Carl Hewitt was an American computer scientist known for creating the **Actor Model** of computation.

## Key Contributions

### Actor Model (1973)

Hewitt introduced the Actor Model as a fundamental model of concurrent computation in which the primitive unit of computation is an "actor" — an entity that can receive messages, send messages, and create other actors.
