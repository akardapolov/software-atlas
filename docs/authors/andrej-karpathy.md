## Biography

Andrej Karpathy is a Slovak-Canadian computer scientist, educator, and
entrepreneur. He was a founding member of OpenAI, director of AI at
Tesla (leading the Autopilot team), and returned to OpenAI before
founding an AI education company in 2024.

Karpathy is widely known for making complex AI concepts accessible
through his blog posts, YouTube videos, and open-source projects.
His "Let's build GPT" video and **nanoGPT** repository are canonical
educational resources for understanding transformers.

## Key Contributions

### nanoGPT (2022)

Karpathy released nanoGPT — a clean, minimal implementation of a
GPT-like transformer in PyTorch, designed for educational purposes.
The codebase is small enough to read in an afternoon but complete
enough to train real models.

### "Let's Build GPT" (2022)

A ~2-hour YouTube video where Karpathy builds a transformer from
scratch. It is widely regarded as the best intuition-building resource
for understanding how LLMs work.

### Tesla Autopilot (2017–2022)

As director of AI at Tesla, Karpathy led the computer vision team
responsible for Autopilot's neural network stack. He pioneered the
use of large-scale data collection and fleet learning for autonomous
driving.

### AI Education

Karpathy's blog posts on recurrent neural networks, convolutional
networks, and reinforcement learning are standard references. His
"Zero to Hero" neural networks series teaches deep learning from
fundamentals.
